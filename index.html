ABOUT ME

I love to learn and I am always open to change!

I am currently a Database specialist at the Society for Mining, Metallurgy, and Exploration in Colorado. A normal day for me is running various reports using MYSQL Server and Business Objects through our interface, Personify. My main objective is to automate reports and information that is clean and reliable. Some of the best parts of my daily work, is to drill down the data. Find out why the data is there, who and when it was put there, where the data should be, etc.

In late August 2019, I graduated from an extremely subversive coding boot camp from the University of Denver. I learned during the course of this boot camp that I enjoyed front-end deployment. The frustrations of lining columns, picking fonts, making responsive screen sizes, are all worth the pain once you deploy your final work.

I was born in Fruita, Colorado, January 17. I graduated in 2009 from Parker, Colorado and spent most of my early 20's in the Service Industry before settling down with my current employer.

I have two Razor Edge Pitbulls that take up the whole bed every night. I would love to tell you that I take full advantage of living in a state full of adventure, but I do not!

I have a huge family and love every minute of it, minus Christmas shopping and remembering birthdays!! We are all very close and we love trying new food and drinks.
